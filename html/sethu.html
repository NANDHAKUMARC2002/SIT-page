SETHU INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

SETHU INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY is an autonomous engineering college, affiliated to Anna University. The institute was established in 1995 and awarded the Autonomous status in 2012 by UGC. It has also been awarded an ‘A++’ grade accreditation by NAAC and is also recognized by the NBA. With a present strength of 4800 students, SIT offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses across varied streams of engineering and sciences. The institute attracts top recruiters from various industry verticals for campus placements.
